About

Information about OLYMPUS and the site

The OLYMPUS Titan Factbook™ is an immersive creative project that presents a fictional United States government database tracking metahumans — titans — operating within its jurisdiction. It is intended as a contribution to a mythological tradition as old as storytelling itself: exploring the values and flaws of society by anthropomorphizing them as extraordinary people with extraordinary abilities, allowing us to examine the underlying mores more readily.

In that tradition, this site is intended as a work of social and cultural scrutinization through the magnifying lens of mythology expressed today in the form of superheroes.
